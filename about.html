Founder

Clint Day

Founder & Lead Dev

Clint is the founder and lead developer behind Ai-Auto, where he focuses on bridging the gap between manual grind and autonomous freedom. A serial entrepreneur, AI enthusiast, and automation expert, he’s spent his career proving one thing: with the right tools, one person can do the work of ten.

As the author of Set Your Own Salary, Clint teaches people how to take control of their income, career trajectory, and personal leverage. His work as a teacher and professor has shaped his mission—helping others understand not only how to use technology, but how to use it to create opportunity, autonomy, and meaningful work.

From sketching the first Ai-Auto concepts in a notebook to deploying full-stack systems that multiply human effort, Clint’s journey has always centered around empowering individuals to lead, build, and design the lives they want—on their own terms.
