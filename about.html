Who’s Behind It

Meet Clint Day

Founder, entrepreneurship evangelist, and Vietnam veteran — building Ai-Auto for operators who’ve already run something.

Clint Day
Founder

Having learned the life-changing power of starting your own business, Clint Day became an entrepreneurship evangelist. It builds self-confidence, generates passion, fulfills purpose, and enables owners to help others.

Entrepreneurship is also the backbone of the U.S. economy — employing over 55% of all workers as a natural job creator. What makes Clint different is he not only lived entrepreneurship as a serial insurance agency founder, but he started teaching others how to do it rather than take early retirement.

He is a Vietnam veteran who helped start three veteran entrepreneurship programs, and actively supports DAV, a 1-million-strong non-profit for disabled vets.

In 2019 Clint was awarded the Silver Star Award for Entrepreneurial Excellence by the President of the National Association for Community College Entrepreneurship. He holds an MBA and five certificates in entrepreneurship including the SEE from Babson College and Entrepreneurship Essentials from the Harvard Business School.

He has been professionally qualified by AACSB, the Association to Advance Collegiate Schools of Business, while his explanations are easy to understand and highly entertaining.
