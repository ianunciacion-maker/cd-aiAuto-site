The Basics

What Is ClawMate?

Built on the open-source OpenClaw engine, ClawMate is the commercial wrapper that turns powerful AI automation into a one-click cloud product. It plans, builds, and manages entire online businesses AND automates your daily operations — all from a single chat thread.

ClawMate

The Business Builder on Autopilot

Powered by OpenClaw's AI engine, ClawMate scans trending niches, creates digital products from scratch, writes conversion-focused sales pages, syndicates content across platforms, manages your calendar, drafts emails in your voice, monitors your sales pipeline, and remembers everything about your business.

Powered by OpenClaw

Built on the same open-source AI agent engine with 85,000+ GitHub stars and 100,000+ Discord members. ClawMate packages this battle-tested technology into a zero-prompt commercial product — no coding, no terminal, no configuration required.

Cloud-Based

Unlike raw OpenClaw (which is self-hosted and technical), ClawMate runs entirely in the cloud, accessible from any device. Your "Messaging Office" — manage your entire business via a single WhatsApp, Slack, or Telegram chat thread.

Always Working

Background Execution means ClawMate keeps building, monitoring, and optimizing even after you log out. Plus Persistent Memory — it stores your business preferences, writing style, and project history, getting smarter about your operations over time. Self-adjusts strategy if something underperforms.

The backstory: The OpenClaw engine (originally called Clawdbot/Moltbot) went viral with small businesses in early 2026, created by Peter Steinberger. With 85,000+ GitHub stars and 100,000+ Discord members, it's one of the most powerful open-source AI agents available. ClawMate, created by Venkatesh Kumar, wraps this engine in a commercial cloud product — adding a visual dashboard, pre-built templates, PageMate page builder, NanoAI Pro design studio, and business automation tools. Available through JVZoo, ClawMate makes OpenClaw's power accessible to non-technical entrepreneurs.

3 Simple Steps

How It Works

You don't need to be technical. ClawMate's zero-prompt intelligence handles everything — here's the three-step process.

01

Activate It

Log into your cloud dashboard and click the "Activate" button. This single action triggers the autonomous AI brain. No terminal, no configuration, no prompt engineering. The onboarding wizard connects your accounts (email, calendar, messaging apps, business tools).

02

Build or Delegate

For new businesses: the Trend-Sensing Engine scans for profitable niches, then auto-generates digital products, sales pages, and marketing assets. For existing businesses: set up your "Messaging Office" — text ClawMate via WhatsApp/Slack/Telegram to delegate tasks like "summarize my emails", "draft an invoice", or "schedule a meeting".

03

Launch & Optimize

One click syndicates content across WordPress, social media, and marketplaces. ClawMate then enters Background Execution mode — monitoring performance, self-adjusting strategy, sending you alerts, and optimizing even after you log out.

Power Tools Need Safety Gear

You wouldn't let an untrained person operate a forklift without a cage and a hard hat. The same logic applies here.

ClawMate is an incredibly powerful tool that can build businesses and manage operations autonomously. But because it can publish content, send messages, and execute on your behalf, you should understand its safeguards.

The good news: ClawMate benefits from both OpenClaw's open-source security track record and its own cloud-hosted protections. You approve before anything goes live.

Your Safety Checklist

  • Review before publishing — ClawMate drafts content and pages for your approval before anything goes live
  • Cloud-hosted security — ClawMate's managed infrastructure handles updates, encryption, and authenticated access
  • OpenClaw community patches — CVEs fixed within 48 hours, 85,000+ developers watching the code
  • No stored payment info — financial transactions are handled by secure third-party processors
  • Content compliance filters — built-in safeguards prevent inappropriate or harmful content generation
  • Revoke access anytime — disconnect accounts or deactivate ClawMate at any time from the dashboard
  • Start with one project — begin with a single product or workflow and expand once you're comfortable

Security Track Record

ClawMate benefits from both worlds: the open-source OpenClaw community's rapid security patching (CVEs fixed within 48 hours, 85,000+ developers watching the code) AND ClawMate's managed cloud infrastructure with automatic updates, encrypted data, and authenticated dashboard access.
